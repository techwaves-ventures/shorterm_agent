Questions hosts ask.

Will this get my account banned?

Shorterm operates your account the way you would — it drafts replies, and a human approves and clicks send on every message. No mass, unattended blasting. It replies only to your real inquiries, personalized, and skips poor fits. Human-in-the-loop is the core design, precisely to stay account-safe.

Is this automated sending?

Checking for new leads is automated (twice daily); sending is not — it's one click by you, per message. You can also send by email in the same click if you choose.

Will it say something wrong to my guest?

It drafts only from the facts you enter in your unit catalog. It won't invent amenities, promise availability, or quote a price you didn't set. You see and can edit every draft before it goes.

What about my credentials and my guests' data?

The hosted version stores your FurnishedFinder email in an encrypted per-tenant vault, isolates tenants, and processes inquiry text only to draft your replies. FurnishedFinder login is passwordless (email + code or magic link) — we never store a password.

Which platforms?

FurnishedFinder today. The adapter model is built to add platforms based on pilot demand.
