Questions hosts ask.

Will this get my FurnishedFinder account in trouble?

Shorterm works your account the way you would: it replies to your real inquiries, personalized, at a human pace — no mass blasting. By default a person approves every message. This is a genuine consideration with any automation, so we're upfront about it during pilot onboarding rather than burying it here.

Does it send on its own?

Only if you turn it on. Out of the box it drafts and schedules everything and waits for your approval. Autopilot is a switch you flip, and even then it's step-by-step: you decide which messages may go unattended. Check-in instructions can never be automated, because they carry access details.

Will it say something wrong to my guest?

It can only state facts from your unit catalog. It won't invent amenities, promise availability, or quote a price you didn't set. If a detail is missing it asks the guest instead of guessing — and it flags the gap to you rather than filling it in.

What happens to my credentials and my guests' data?

Your FurnishedFinder email is encrypted at rest in a per-tenant vault and tenants are isolated from each other. Login is passwordless, so there's no password to store. Inquiry text is used only to draft your replies.

Which platforms does it support?

FurnishedFinder today. The adapter model is built to add Airbnb, VRBO and Booking.com — pilot demand decides the order.
